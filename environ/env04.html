Important places in Yohuala

Remnants of Lake Atoccan:

This is one of the many lakes that make up the remnants of lake Atoccan. Located just 10 miles east of the country's capital. The lake has been drained for the past few centuries since the Spanish conquest. Now all
that remains are a series of swamps, rivers, and small lakes dotted around the land.

Veratumba:

This is one of the most important ports in all of Yohuala. As the majority of our
imports enter through this single port. Everything abroad enters here.
Goods, services, ships, people, faiths, armies, nations, flags, ..... Everything comes here.
